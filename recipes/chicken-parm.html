Chicken Parmesan

A classic Italian-American chicken dish

Chicken Parmesan, also known as Chicken Parmigana, is a classic Italian chicken dish that's usually served with Italian pastas or on a sandwich. It's fried breaded chicken with marinara sauce and melted cheese on top. This recipe takes around 30-40 minutes to make.

Ingredients

Marinara Sauce Ingredients

  • 1 28oz can of San Marzano tomatoes, crushed
  • 4 thick slices of thick bacon
  • 5 cloves of garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Pinch of sugar
  • 1/2 bunch thyme

Chicken Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 2 cups of panko bread crumbs
  • 4 eggs plus splash of water
  • 4 cups of mozerella, grated
  • 1 cup Parmigiano Reggiano

Steps

  1. Slice thick cut bacon into 1/2 inch wide pieces. Toss into cold pan with the olive oil and place over medium heat, stirring often.
  2. Cook the bacon until crispy and add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes to pan. Continue cooking on medium heat until toasted and fragrant.
  3. Scoop out the solids and leave oil in the pan. Finely chop the solids on a cutting board and add it back to the pan along with the crushed San Marzano tomatoes. Season with salt and pepper to taste and a pinch of sugar. Combine everything and bring it to a simmer. Stir occasionally for 15 minutes.
  4. While simmering, add the thyme and grated Parmigiano Reggiano cheese. Sitr until fully combined and set the sauce on the side.
  5. In seperate bowls, place panko crumbs, beaten eggs with a splash of water, and flour whisked with salt and pepper.
  6. Toss the chicken in the flour, dip in egg wash to coat thoroughly, then toss in the panko crumbs and coat thoroughly while pressing into the chicken.
  7. Fill the pan with oil about halfway and heat it over medium heat until it reaches 350 F degrees. Fry the chicken on 1 side for 4 minutes, flip and fry the other side for 4 minutes.
  8. Mix the grated parmigiano reggiano and mozerella cheese together. After frying the chicken and setting it aside, cover the chicken generously with marinara sauce and then the cheese mixture.
  9. Place chicken in a broiler for 7 minutes or untill the cheese is melted or golden brown.
