Zuppa Toscana

Zuppa Toscana is an Italian soup dish originating from Tuscany, Italy.

Zuppa Toscana is a delicious Italian soup usually made with bacon, hot sausage, potatoes, and kale. This soup is usally served as an appetizer before the main course or it can enjoyed by itself with some garlic bread. This recipe takes around 30-40 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 6 oz bacon, chopped
  • 1 lb Hot Italian sausage
  • 10 garlic cloves, pressed
  • 1 medium onion, finely diced
  • 6 cups of chicken broth
  • 4 cups of water
  • 5 medium russet potatoes, chopped into 1/4 inch pieces
  • 1 kale bundle
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese to serve

Steps

  1. In a large pot, add chopped bacon over medium-high heat and sauté until browned. Remove bacon and place it on a paper-lined plate. Scoop out exceess oil, leaving 1tbsp in the pot.
  2. Break up Italian sausage and add it to the pot, sauté until cooked through. Remove to paper-lined plate.
  3. Add diced onion to the pot and sauté until soft and golden. Then add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
  4. Add chicken broth and water and bring to boil. Add sliced potatoes and cook until easily pierced with fork.
  5. Right before potatoes are done, add chopped kale and cooked sauasge and bring everything to a light boil.
  6. Add heavy cream and bring to a boil, stir everything. Season with salt and pepper and remove from heat.
